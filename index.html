EXTRA INFO

Nothing to do on Saturday 16 January 2021 or Sunday 17 January 2021?
Feel free to come to our awesome western party with activities that
bring back the lifestyle of a native American cowboy. Activities such
as rodeo riding, line dancing or shooting in a VR world that looks like
a real western world. If you are a person that doesn’t like active
activities, we also have western art on our walls and of course you
can also watch the first episode of Godless with the cast. If you want
to drink or eat something you can always visit our saloon. We serve
typical western food and drinks.

DISNEY PARTY

Two years ago we received the request to organize a Disney
wedding and we made a whole world in Disney style. That
began outside the building with the story of the two people
that had their wedding on that day. The experience
continues indoors with booths that each have a unique
theme and activity.

60S PARTY

One and a half year ago we received the request to make
an event in the style of the sixties because they missed
that time. So bring back that time for them. From the clothes
to the walls to the tables, … everything was in that theme
and yes, afterwards they told us that they had forgotten
that it was the year 2019.
